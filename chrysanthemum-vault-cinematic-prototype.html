About the Vault

Chrysanthemum Vault exists for those who understand that true luxury is not loud. It is the quiet pleasure of holding something that has survived time, taste, and trend.

We acquire, conserve, and place fewer than twenty-five objects each year. Each piece is accompanied by a private ledger of provenance and care.

1. Discovery — We travel quietly and listen.
2. Vetting — Every object is examined by three independent specialists.
3. Placement — Matched only with collectors who will honor its story.

Private viewings by appointment only.

The Vision

A curated vault, not a storefront.

Chrysanthemum Vault is built as a quiet digital gallery for paintings, marquetry, object-work, jewelry, records, and selected commissions. The landing page uses a black field, a gold horizon, and architectural linework to make each collection feel discovered rather than advertised.

The Alchemy

Material, hand, and transformation.

The site language is restrained: gold line, dark ground, measured typography, and portal-like transitions. The visual system supports work made through paint, wood, stone, metal, archive, and careful assembly.

The Collection

Six entrances into the vault.

Paintings, marquetry, curiosities, jewelry, archive, and commissions are presented as separate repositories. Each category can later become a full gallery page without changing the landing-page structure.

The Atelier

The working room behind the object.

The Atelier section is reserved for process notes, material records, provenance language, conservation notes, and the quieter written context behind the work.
